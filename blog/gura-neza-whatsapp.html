Gura neza kuri WhatsApp na Instagram mu Rwanda

Yanditswe na Irene Bahati · 4 Werurwe 2026 · Gusoma: 9 min

WhatsApp na Instagram byabaye nk'isoko nini mu Rwanda. Buri munsi, abacuruzi ibihumbi bashyira ibicuruzwa ku Instagram stories, kuri WhatsApp status, no muri groups za WhatsApp. Imyenda, phones, ibikoresho by'amazu, ibinyobwa, ibindi byinshi — byose bihari.

Ni byiza kubera ko ubucuruzi bworoshye. Umucuruzi ashyira amafoto, uri interesanyi wandika "PM", muganira, ukohereza amafaranga kuri MoMo. Ariko hari ikibazo kinini:

Iyo wohereje amafaranga kuri MoMo y'umuntu ku giti cye, ntakintu gishobora kukurinda. Niba ari umuriganya, amafaranga yawe yagiye — nta subira inyuma.

Muri iyi nkuru, tugiye kukwereka inama 7 z'ingenzi cyane zo kukwirinda igihe uguze kuri WhatsApp na Instagram mu Rwanda, n'uburyo TandPay ishobora kugufasha.

Uko ubucuruzi bwa social commerce bukura mu Rwanda

Gucuruza kuri WhatsApp mu Rwanda byabaye bisanzwe cyane, ariko bikeneye umutekano. Mbere y'uko dutera ku nama, reka turebe impamvu iyi ngingo ari iy'ingenzi cyane muri iki gihe.

Mu Rwanda, abantu barenga miliyoni 4 bakoresha internet, kandi abenshi muri bo bakoresha WhatsApp n'Instagram buri munsi. Ubucuruzi bwa social commerce burimo gukura ku kigero kitangaje — hari abacuruzi b'imyenda i Kigali bakoresha Instagram gusa, nta boutique bafite, bakunguka neza.

Ariko uko ubucuruzi bukura, n'abariganya bariyongera. Abantu benshi baratakaje amafaranga mu gura kuri WhatsApp — baguze phones ntibazibone, baguze imyenda ibageraho itari iyo bari bateganije, cyangwa umucuruzi akababuramo nyuma yo kwishyura.

None se wakwirinda ute? Dore inama 7 zifatika.

Inama ya 1: Reba reviews n'amakuru y'umucuruzi

Mbere yo kugura ku mucuruzi uwo ari we wese kuri social media, ukore ubushakashatsi buke. Ibaze ibibazo nk'ibi:

  • Ese uyu mucuruzi afite followers bangahe? Ni ba nyabo? Cyangwa ni followers z'ibinyoma?
  • Ese abantu banditse reviews (amanota) ku bintu baguze?
  • Ese umucuruzi yerekanye amafoto y'ibicuruzwa nyayo, cyangwa ni amafoto yavuye kuri Google?
  • Ese afite izina ry'ubucuruzi rizwi cyangwa akorera ku izina rye bwite gusa?
  • Ese abaguzi be babibona neza? Baza mu comments.

Umucuruzi wizewe akunze kugira reviews z'abaguzi be bashimiye, amafoto nyayo y'ibicuruzwa (atari amafoto yo kuri Google), n'aho umubona (adresse cyangwa numero ya telefoni).

Ikimenyetso cyiza

Niba umucuruzi akoresha TandPay, ni ikimenyetso cyiza cy'uko ari umucuruzi wizewe. Kubera ko yakemera gukoresha escrow, bisobanura ko atigeze atinya ko amafaranga ye azamufatirwa — azi ko azagutumira ibicuruzwa nyabyo.

Inama ya 2: Ntukoreshe MoMo y'umuntu ku giti cye — koresha escrow

Iyi ni inama y'ingenzi cyane muri iyi nkuru yose.

Iyo wohereje amafaranga kuri MoMo y'umuntu ku giti cye, uwakoze transaction ni wowe n'uwo muntu gusa. Nta rundi ruhande rw'imbere. Nta "undo" button. Niba uwo muntu ari umuriganya, amafaranga yagiye.

Ahubwo, koresha escrow. Escrow ni uburyo aho amafaranga yawe afatwa n'urundi ruhande rw'imbere (nka TandPay) kugeza igihe ibicuruzwa byakugejejweho. Umutekano wo kwishyura kuri MoMo uterwa n'uko amafaranga afatwa mu buryo bwizewe — ni yo mpamvu escrow ari ingenzi.

Icyo bisobanura: aho kohereza 50,000 RWF kuri MoMo ya Jean (utazi niba azakutumira ibicuruzwa), wishyura binyuze kuri TandPay. Amafaranga afatwa mu mutekano. Jean atumira ibicuruzwa, akugezaho code y'imibare 4, uyandika — amafaranga amugereho. Ariko niba Jean atarakoze delivery, amafaranga aragaruka.

Ntukigere ukora ibi

Ntukigere wohereza amafaranga kuri MoMo y'umuntu utazi ku giti cye, cyane cyane ku bintu bihenze (birenze 5,000 RWF). Ntakintu kirinda amafaranga yawe muri uwo murongo.

Inama ya 3: Koresha TandPay ku bintu birenze 5,000 RWF

Kuri ibintu bike — urugero, igikombe cy'icyayi cya 500 RWF — birashoboka ko escrow idasabwa. Ariko ku kintu cyose kirenze 5,000 RWF, escrow ni ngombwa.

Ibuka ko amafaranga ya serivisi ya TandPay ari 2.5% gusa ku muguzi. Ku kintu cy'amafaranga 10,000 RWF, wishyura 250 RWF gusa y'umutekano. Ni igiciro gito cyane ugereranyije no gutakaza 10,000 RWF yose niba wariganyijwe.

Inama ya 4: Saba amafoto y'ibicuruzwa arimo izina ryabo ryanditswe ku mpapuro

Uburyo bumwe bworoshye bwo kumenya niba umucuruzi afite ibicuruzwa nyabyo ni ukumusaba gufotora ibicuruzwa byihariye — atari amafoto yo kuri Google cyangwa andi mashusho yavuye ku zindi pages.

Mumusabe agufotorere ibicuruzwa hamwe n'impapuro yanditsemo izina rye (cyangwa izina ry'ubucuruzi bwe) n'itariki. Ibyo biguha icyizere ko ibicuruzwa bihari koko, ko ari ibye, kandi ko bifotowe none.

Umucuruzi wizewe ntazahakana iyo nyandiko. Uwanga — banza wibaze impamvu.

Inama ya 5: Niba igiciro ari cyiza cyane, ubanze wibaze

Mu Kinyarwanda, twibuka ko "ibyiza bitagira akamaro ni ibya setan." Iyi mvugo isa n'iy'ukuri cyane mu bucuruzi bwa kuri internet.

Niba umucuruzi agurisha iPhone 14 ku 50,000 RWF kandi iyo phone ihenze 400,000+ RWF ku isoko, ni ikimenyetso cy'uburiganya. Nta mucuruzi wagurisha igicuruzwa ku giciro gito cyane atabimenye — cyangwa se hari ikibazo.

Ibaze ibi mbere yo kwishyura

Kubera iki iyi phone ihendutse cyane kuruta aho biyigurishiriza hose? Ese hari ikintu kitameze neza? Niba igiciro ari cyiza cyane kugira ngo kibe ukuri — birashoboka ko atari ukuri.

Ibicuruzwa by'ibinyoma cyangwa ibicuruzwa by'ubwangizi (stolen goods) nawo akenshi biguruka ku giciro gito cyane. Kwirinda ni ukumenya ko igiciro cyiza cyane akenshi gifite ikibazo inyuma.

Inama ya 6: Bika ibimenyetso — screenshots z'ibiganiro n'ubwishyu

Buri gihe uguze kuri internet, screenshot byose:

  • Ikiganiro (chat) n'umucuruzi — message byose, amasezerano, igiciro mwumvikanyeho
  • Amafoto y'ibicuruzwa yagushyize
  • Icyemezo cya MoMo (transaction confirmation)
  • Izina n'inomero y'umucuruzi
  • Profile ye ya Instagram/WhatsApp

Niba hari ikibazo mu nyuma, aya makuru azagufasha mu gutanga raporo kuri RIB (Rwanda Investigation Bureau) cyangwa mu gufungura dispute kuri TandPay.

Kuri TandPay, ibimenyetso byose (amafoto, ibiganiro, amakuru y'ubwishyu) bibikwa muri system, bityo ntukeneye gukora screenshots byinshi — ariko ku bundi buryo bwo kwishyura, ni wowe ubikurikirana.

Inama ya 7: Tanga raporo — RIB, MTN fraud line

Niba wariganyijwe, ntutinye gutanga raporo. Benshi batinya kubera batekereza ko ntacyo bifasha, ariko si ukuri. Gutanga raporo bigufasha wowe n'abandi:

  • RIB (Rwanda Investigation Bureau) — Gana kuri poste ya polisi hafi yawe cyangwa hamagara RIB. Bafite igice gihariye cya cybercrime.
  • MTN Fraud Line — Hamagara *133# cyangwa ujye kuri MTN service center hafi yawe utange raporo.
  • Kuri TandPay — Niba waguze binyuze kuri TandPay, fungura dispute muri system. Dufite team ikemura ibibazo mu masaha 24.

Iyo utanze raporo, ugufasha wowe ubwawe (hari amahirwe yo gusubizwa amafaranga) kandi ugufasha n'abandi (RIB ishobora gufata umuriganya mbere yuko yiganya abandi).

Uko TandPay ikemura ikibazo cy'icyizere

Ikibazo kinini cy'ubucuruzi bwa social commerce ni icyizere. Umuguzi ntiyizera umucuruzi, umucuruzi nawe atiyizera umuguzi. TandPay ikemura iki kibazo mu buryo bworoshye:

  1. Umucuruzi azi ko amafaranga ahari — umuguzi yishyuye. Ntashobora kwanga delivery.
  2. Umuguzi azi ko amafaranga ye arindwe — azasubizwa niba umucuruzi atakoze delivery.
  3. TandPay iri hagati, nk'umucamanza wizewe, ifata icyemezo niba hari ikibazo.

Ibyo bisobanura ko umuguzi n'umucuruzi bashobora gucuruza batanategerezanya — kandi buri wese arindwe.

Byongeye kandi, TandPay itanga amanota y'icyizere (trust score) ku mucuruzi. Uko umucuruzi arangiza transactions nyinshi neza, ni ko amanota ye yiyongera. Umuguzi ashobora kubona aya manota mbere yo kwishyura — biramufasha kumenya niba umucuruzi ari umwizirwa.

Intambwe 3 zo gukoresha TandPay

Gukoresha TandPay ni byoroshye cyane:

  1. Umucuruzi akora konte kuri app.tandpay.com, ashyiramo ibicuruzwa, akora commande.
  2. Umuguzi akanda link umucuruzi yamoherereje kuri WhatsApp, areba ibicuruzwa, yishyura kuri MoMo.
  3. Delivery igihe umucuruzi agejeje ibicuruzwa, umuguzi yandika code y'imibare 4, amafaranga arekurirwa umucuruzi.

Nta gahunda ya software yo gushyiramo. Nta frais z'ukwiyandikisha. Nta kintu kitoroshye.

Incamake: Gura neza, gura n'umutekano

Ubucuruzi bwa social commerce ni bwiza — bubangikanye n'ubucuruzi bw'umubiri mu buryo bworoshye kandi bwihuse. Ariko budakoresha uburyo bwizewe bwo kwishyura, buri muguzi aba ari mu kaga. Kwishyura neza kuri internet bisaba ko ukoresha igikoresho cy'umutekano nka escrow.
