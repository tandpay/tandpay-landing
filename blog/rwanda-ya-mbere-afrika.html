Rwanda Ni ya Mbere mu Afrika mu Bucuruzi — TandPay Ifasha Abacuruzi Gukoresha Iyo Nyungu

Yanditswe na Irene Bahati · 7 Mutarama 2026 · Gusoma: 9 min

Mu mwaka wa 2024, Banki y'Isi (World Bank) yasohoje raporo yayo nshya izwi nka B-READY — igipimo gipima uko ibihugu bimeze ku isoko ry'ubucuruzi. Mu bihugu byose by'Afurika, Rwanda ni yo ya mbere (World Bank B-READY 2024-2025). Mu isi yose, Rwanda iri mu bihugu 30 bya mbere.

Ibi si ibintu byoroshye. Bisobanura ko Rwanda ifite amategeko meza y'ubucuruzi, infrastructure ikomeye, n'urwego rwa fintech rurimo gukura cyane. Ni impamvu abashoramari bo ku isi bakunda u Rwanda, kandi ni impamvu ubukungu bw'u Rwanda bukura ku kigero cya 8.9% buri mwaka.

Ariko hari ikibazo kimwe gikomeye kiri hagati y'iyi nzozi n'ubucuruzi bwa buri munsi: 42% by'abaguzi mu Rwanda batinya uburiganya bw'abacuruzi (TransUnion 2024). Rwanda yubatse umuhanda — ariko abagenda kuri uwo muhanda baracyatinya.

TandPay iza gukemura iki kibazo. Rwanda yubatse umuhanda. TandPay yongeraho imikandara.

Imibare y'ubucuruzi bw'u Rwanda

#1 mu Afrika kuri B-READY (World Bank 2024-2025)
8.9% ubwiyongere bw'ubukungu (GDP growth)
75+ fintechs ziyandikishijwe (kuva kuri 3 mu 2014)

Reka turebe neza ibyo iyi mibare isobanura:

  • #1 mu Afrika kuri B-READY. Ibi bisobanura ko nta handi hantu mu Afurika gutangira ubucuruzi byoroshye nko mu Rwanda. Amategeko ari meza, kwiyandikisha ni bworoshye, kandi guverinoma ifasha abacuruzi.
  • 8.9% GDP growth. Ubukungu bw'u Rwanda burimo gukura cyane — buri mwaka. Ibyo bisobanura ko hari amahirwe mashya y'ubucuruzi buri gihe.
  • 75+ fintechs. Mu 2014, Rwanda yari ifite fintechs 3 gusa. Ubu hafi ya 75+. Ibyo byerekana ko u Rwanda ari ahantu heza h'ikoranabuhanga ry'amafaranga.
  • 96% financial inclusion. Hafi ya buri Munyarwanda wese afite uburyo bwo gukoresha serivisi z'amafaranga (FinScope 2024). Mu 2008, uwo mubare wari 48% gusa.
  • 86% mobile money adoption. Mu bihugu byose ku isi, Rwanda iri mu byafite abakoresha mobile money benshi cyane.

Iyi mibare yerekana ko Rwanda ifite infrastructure ikomeye y'ubucuruzi bwa digitale. Ariko infrastructure ni intambwe imwe gusa. Intambwe ya kabiri ni icyizere — kumenya ko igihe ucuruza kuri internet, amafaranga yawe arizewe.

Ikibazo: Infrastructure nziza ariko icyizere gito

Tekereza ibi: Rwanda ifite umuhanda mwiza wa Kigali — Butare. Amodoka zigenda neza, umuhanda ni umusanzu. Ariko tekereza ko 42% by'abantu batinya kugenda kuri uwo muhanda kubera impanuka. Ntibavuga ko umuhanda ari mubi — bavuga ko nta mikandara iri mu modoka.

Ibyo ni byo bibaho mu bucuruzi bwa digitale mu Rwanda. Infrastructure ni nziza: mobile money, internet, amategeko. Ariko 42% by'abaguzi batinya uburiganya bw'abacuruzi (TransUnion 2024). Ntibavuga ko system ari mbi — bavuga ko nta buryo bwo kwirinda mu transaction imwe imwe.

Rwanda yubatse umuhanda. TandPay yongeraho imikandara.

Umuhanda (infrastructure) uraho. System y'amafaranga (mobile money) iraho. Amategeko (regulation) araho. Icyo gikeneye gusa ni umutekano mu bucuruzi bwa buri munsi — kumenya ko iyo wishyuye, uzabona ibyo waguze. TandPay itanga uwo mutekano binyuze muri escrow.

Abacuruzi 241,000 bakeneye TandPay

Ubushakashatsi bwa NISR (National Institute of Statistics of Rwanda) bwerekana ko mu Rwanda hari hafi ya 241,000 ubucuruzi buto n'ubucuruzi bw'umwimerere. Benshi muri bo bacuruza kuri WhatsApp na Instagram — batangiza ibicuruzwa, baganira n'abaguzi, bakishyurwa kuri MoMo.

Buri wese muri abo bacuruzi 241,000 ahura n'ikibazo kimwe: "Nishyura mbere cyangwa ntumire mbere?" Umuguzi ntashaka kwishyura mbere y'uko abona ibicuruzwa. Umucuruzi ntashaka gutumira mbere y'uko yishyuriwe. Iki kiganiro gica ibikorwa — kandi hari igihe abakiriya bagenda kubera ntawe ushaka gutangira.

TandPay ikuraho iyi mpaka burundu. Umuguzi yishyura mu escrow — amafaranga afatwa mu mutekano, ariko umucuruzi ntashobora kuyakoresha kugeza delivery irangiye. Umucuruzi atumira, atanga code y'imibare 4, umuguzi ayandika, amafaranga arekurirwa umucuruzi. Ntawe usabwa kwizera undi — system ndiyo irinda bombi.

Rwanda imaze gutsinda — ubu ni igihe cyo kwirinda
