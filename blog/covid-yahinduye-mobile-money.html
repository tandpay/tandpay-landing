Uko COVID-19 Yahinduye Mobile Money mu Rwanda — N'impamvu TandPay Ari Intambwe Ikurikira

Yanditswe na Irene Bahati · 11 Mutarama 2026 · Gusoma: 9 min

Mu ntangiriro za 2020, COVID-19 yahinduriye byose mu Rwanda. Amaduka yarapfunzwe. Amasoko arapfungwa. Abantu ntibashobore guhura nk'uko bisanzwe. Ariko ubuzima bwagombaga gukomeza — abantu bagombaga kugura ibiryo, kwishyura serivisi, no kohereza amafaranga ku miryango yabo.

Icyo gihe, mobile money yarahindutse igikoresho cy'ingenzi cy'ubuzima bwa buri munsi. Abantu batabigeze bakoresha bagize bwa mbere bakoresha MTN MoMo. Abari bakoresha bakiyongera ku mitumire. Mu mezi 3 gusa, abantu 700,000 bashya binjiye muri mobile money mu Rwanda (Cenfri 2020).

Ubu, nyuma y'imyaka 6, mobile money yarahindutse imbaragarwanda y'ubukungu bw'u Rwanda. Ariko hari ikibazo gishya cyabonetse hamwe n'ubwo gukura: uburiganya mu bucuruzi bwa kuri internet bwiyongereye cyane. N'impamvu TandPay ari intambwe ikurikira — uburyo bwo kurinda amafaranga yawe mu bucuruzi bwa digitale.

Imibare y'impinduka: Mbere na nyuma ya COVID

700,000 abakoresha bashya mu mezi 3 (Cenfri 2020)
+450% ubwiyongere bw'ibyaguzwe kuri mobile money
62%→86% ubwiyongere bw'abakoresha mobile money (FinScope)

Mbere ya COVID-19, mu Rwanda hari abantu 62% bakoresha mobile money (FinScope 2016). Mu 2024, uwo mubare wari ugeze kuri 86% (FinScope 2024). Iryo yongereye ryatewe n'ibintu bitatu by'ingenzi:

  • Guhanagura amafaranga y'ikoreshwa (fee waiver). BNR na MTN bafashanye gukuraho amafaranga y'ikoreshwa rya MoMo mu gihe cya COVID, bituma abantu bose bashobora gukoresha mobile money ubuntu. Ibi byatumye abantu benshi bwa mbere bakoresha MoMo.
  • Kwirinda gufata amafaranga y'impapuro. Abantu batinya gufata "cash" kubera virusi, bituma bakoresha digitale aho gukoresha impapuro z'amafaranga.
  • Ubucuruzi bwimukiye kuri internet. Abacuruzi batabigeze bakoresha internet batangiye gucuruza kuri WhatsApp na Instagram kubera amaduka yari apfunzwe.

Iyi mpinduka yari nziza cyane. Abantu benshi babonye uburyo bworoshye bwo kwishyura no kohereza amafaranga. Ariko hamwe n'ubwo gukura, ikibazo gishya cyabonetse: uburiganya mu bucuruzi bwa kuri internet.

Igihe mobile money yakuraga, uburiganya nabwo bwiyongeraga

Iki ni ikintu abantu benshi batazi: uko abakoresha mobile money biyongera, abagambanyi nabo biyongera. Ubushakashatsi bwa BNR na CGAP bwerekana imibare iterwa ubwoba:

84% by'abakoresha mobile money barigeze gukorerwa uburiganya (BNR/CGAP)
44% by'abahohotewe batakaje amafaranga (BNR/CGAP)
42% by'abaguzi batinya uburiganya bw'abacuruzi (TransUnion 2024)

84% by'abakoresha mobile money mu Rwanda barigeze gukorerwa uburiganya ubwo ari bwo bwose — guhamagara gushuka, SMS z'ibinyoma, cyangwa gucuruzwa ibintu bidahari. Muri bo, 44% batakaje amafaranga nyayo. Ibyo ni hafi kimwe cya kabiri by'abahohotewe.

Impamvu ni izi: mobile money yagenewe kohereza amafaranga, ntiyagenewe gucuruza. Iyo wohereje amafaranga ku muntu ku giti cye kuri MoMo ugura ikintu, nta buryo bwo kuyasubizwa niba uwagurishije atakugiriye neza. Transaction yarangiye — amafaranga yawe yagiye.

Intambwe COVID yateye — n'intambwe idakiri buhari

Reka turebe uko impinduka zabaye:

Intambwe ya 1: Amafaranga y'impapuro → Mobile money. Iyi ntambwe yarakoze neza. Abantu bahindukiye gukoresha MoMo aho gukoresha cash. Guverinoma, BNR, na MTN bafashanye kuyituma iba intambwe yoroshye kandi ihendutse.

Intambwe ya 2: Mobile money → Ubucuruzi bwa digitale. Iyi ntambwe nayo irimo gukora. Abantu barimo gucuruza kuri WhatsApp, Instagram, na Facebook bakoresha MoMo kwishyura.

Intambwe ya 3: Ubucuruzi bwa digitale → Ubucuruzi bwizewe bwa digitale. Iyi ni intambwe idakiri buhari. Abantu bacuruza kuri internet, ariko ntibafite uburyo bwo kurinda amafaranga yabo. Ni nko kujya mu muhanda utambaye umukandara.

Ikintu cy'ingenzi cyane COVID yigishije u Rwanda ni iki: Abanyarwanda bashobora guhinduka vuba. Mu mezi 3 gusa, miliyoni z'abantu bahindukiye burundu gukoresha mobile money. Ntabwo byari byoroshye — byasabye kwiga ikintu gishya, kwizera system nshya, no guhindura imyitwarire.
