Escrow ni iki? Menya uko TandPay irinda amafaranga yawe

Yanditswe na Irene Bahati · 4 Werurwe 2026 · Gusoma: 8 min

Ese waba warigeze wohereza amafaranga kuri MoMo ugura ikintu kuri WhatsApp, hanyuma uwagurishije akaguhesha intege nke, akirengagiza message zawe, cyangwa akagutumira ibintu bitari ibyo wari wisabiye? Niba ari byo, si wowe wenyine. Ibi bibaho buri munsi mu Rwanda, cyane cyane mu bucuruzi bwa social commerce.

Ariko hari uburyo bwo kwikinga: escrow. Escrow mu Rwanda ni uburyo bushya bwo kurinda amafaranga yawe igihe ucuruza kuri internet. Muri iyi nkuru, tugiye kukusobanurira neza escrow ari iki, uburyo ikora, n'uburyo TandPay iyikoresha kugira ngo amafaranga yawe arindwe igihe uguze kuri internet.

Escrow ni iki mu magambo yoroshye?

Tekereza ko ufite inshuti yawe yizewe. Uyu munsi ushaka kugura isaha kuri umucuruzi utazi kuri Instagram. Aho kohereza amafaranga ku mucuruzi utazi, uha inshuti yawe amafaranga, uyibwira uti: "Bika aya mafaranga. Umucuruzi naza kuntumira isaha kandi nkayibona, umubwire abone amafaranga. Ariko niba atayitumye, ungarurire amafaranga yanjye."

Ibyo ni byo escrow ari byo. Ni uburyo bwo gufata amafaranga y'umuguzi, ukayabika ahantu hizewe, kugeza igihe ibicuruzwa byageze ku muguzi.

Escrow = Inshuti yizewe ifata amafaranga kugeza ubonye ibyo waguze. TandPay ni iyo nshuti, ariko ikoresha ikoranabuhanga rya digitale.

Mu by'ukuri, escrow ni ijambo ry'Icyongereza risobanura "gufata ikintu mu mutekano kugeza igihe ibisabwa byuzuzwa." Mu bucuruzi, bisobanura ko amafaranga y'umuguzi afatwa n'urundi ruhande (escrow provider) kugeza igihe umucuruzi yuzuza ibyo yari yaravuze — ni ukuvuga gutumira ibicuruzwa.

Ikibazo: Uburiganya mu bucuruzi bwa kuri internet mu Rwanda

Ubucuruzi bwa social commerce burimo gukura cyane mu Rwanda. Abantu benshi bacuruza kuri WhatsApp, Instagram, TikTok, na Facebook. Ariko uko bukura, n'abagambanyi bariyongera.

1/10 abantu babaye abahohotewe by'uburiganya
84% by'abakoresha mobile money mu Rwanda barigeze gukorerwa uburiganya

Ikibazo kinini ni iki: iyo wohereje amafaranga kuri MoMo ku muntu ku giti cye, nta buryo bworoshye bwo kuyasubizwa. MTN MoMo ni uburyo bwiza bwo kohereza amafaranga, ariko ntibwagenewe kuba uburyo bwo kwishyura ibicuruzwa. Nta buyer protection irimo.

Ibyo ni byo TandPay yaje gukemura.

Uko social commerce ikura mu Rwanda

Mbere y'uko tubona uko TandPay ikora, reka turebe impamvu social commerce ari ikibazo gikomeye muri iki gihe.

Mu Rwanda, abantu benshi ntibakoresha websites nka Amazon cyangwa Jumia. Ahubwo, bacuruza aho babonana: kuri WhatsApp groups, Instagram stories, TikTok live, na Facebook Marketplace. Umucuruzi ashyira amafoto y'ibicuruzwa, abantu bandika "PM" (Private Message), baganira ku giciro, hanyuma umuguzi yohereza amafaranga kuri MoMo.

Ubu buryo ni bwiza kubera ko ari bworoshye — nta site internet usabwa, nta app yihariye. Ariko hari ikibazo: nta kintu kirinda umuguzi. Iyo wohereje amafaranga, uba wizeye umucuruzi gusa. Niba ari umuriganya, amafaranga yawe yagiye.

Uko TandPay ikora: intambwe ku ntambwe

TandPay yashyizweho gukemura iki kibazo nyirizina. Dore uko ikora intambwe ku ntambwe:

Intambwe ya 1: Umucuruzi akora commande

Umucuruzi yinjira kuri TandPay (app.tandpay.com), yandika amakuru y'ibicuruzwa: izina, igiciro, incamake, n'amafoto. Akora commande (order) muri system.

Intambwe ya 2: Agasangiza link y'ubwishyu kuri WhatsApp

TandPay itanga umucuruzi link y'ubwishyu yihariye. Iyi link ayohereza ku muguzi kuri WhatsApp, SMS, cyangwa undi murongo uwo ari wo wose. Umuguzi ntasabwa kugira konte cyangwa gufungura app — akanda link gusa.

Intambwe ya 3: Umuguzi abona amakuru y'ibicuruzwa n'umucuruzi

Iyo umuguzi akanze link, abona page yerekanye ibicuruzwa, igiciro, amafoto, n'amakuru y'umucuruzi (izina, nimero ya telefoni, amanota y'icyizere). Umuguzi ashobora kumenya neza icyo agura n'uwamugurisha mbere yo kwishyura.

Intambwe ya 4: Umuguzi yishyura kuri MTN MoMo

Umuguzi akanda "Ishyura" (Pay), akandika nimero ye ya MoMo. TandPay yohereza MoMo payment request kuri telefoni ye. Yemeza, amafaranga asohoroka kuri MoMo ye.

Ikintu cy'ingenzi cyane

Aya mafaranga ntabwo ajya ku mucuruzi. Ajya kuri konte ya TandPay, aho afatwa mu mutekano (escrow) kugeza igihe ibicuruzwa byageze ku muguzi. Ni iki gituma escrow itandukanye n'ukohereza amafaranga kuri MoMo bisanzwe.

Intambwe ya 5: Amafaranga afatwa na TandPay mu mutekano

Aya mafaranga afatwe ahantu hizewe. Umucuruzi ntashobora kuyakoresha. Umuguzi ntashobora kuyasubiza. Bifatanye kugeza igihe intambwe ikurikira yuzuzwa.

Intambwe ya 6: Umucuruzi ashyikiriza ibicuruzwa hamwe na code y'imibare 4

Umucuruzi atumira ibicuruzwa ku muguzi (delivery). Igihe abigezaho, amwereka code y'imibare 4 yanditswe kuri order muri TandPay. Iyi code ni nk'icyemezo cy'uko ibicuruzwa byashyikirijwe.

Intambwe ya 7: Umuguzi yandika code, amafaranga arekurirwa umucuruzi

Umuguzi areba ibicuruzwa. Niba yishimiye, yandika code y'imibare 4 kuri page ya TandPay. Icyo gihe, amafaranga arekurirwa umucuruzi kuri MoMo ye — bidatinze.

Niba umuguzi adashimishijwe n'ibicuruzwa, ashobora gufungura ikibazo (dispute) muri amasaha 12 ahabwa. TandPay ireba ikibazo, igafata icyemezo.

Icyo ugomba kumenya

Umuguzi afite amasaha 12 yo gutanga raporo niba hari ikibazo. Nyuma y'amasaha 12, niba nta kibazo cyatanzwe, amafaranga arekurirwa umucuruzi mu buryo bw'ibisanzwe.

Amafaranga ya serivisi ya TandPay

TandPay ikoresha amafaranga make cyane kugira ngo buri wese abyishyure:

2.5% Umuguzi yishyura (ku giciro cy'ibicuruzwa)
2.5% Umucuruzi yishyura (ku giciro cy'ibicuruzwa)
5% Igiteranyo (ku giciro cy'ibicuruzwa)

Urugero: Niba uguze ikintu cy'amafaranga 10,000 RWF, wishyura 10,250 RWF (10,000 + 250 ya serivisi). Umucuruzi we abona 9,750 RWF (10,000 - 250 ya serivisi). TandPay ibona 500 RWF muri iyo transaction.

Ibi biratinyuka ugereranyije n'igiciro ushobora gutakaza (amafaranga yawe yose) niba wariganyijwe mu gura bisanzwe kuri MoMo.

Kubera iki TandPay itandukanye n'ibindi?

Hari uburyo bwinshi bwo kohereza amafaranga mu Rwanda — MoMo, banki, cash. Ariko nta na bumwe muri ubwo buryo butanga buyer protection mu social commerce. Dore icyo TandPay itanga:

  • Amafaranga afatwa mu mutekano — nta wushobora kuyakoresha kugeza igihe umucuruzi atumiye ibicuruzwa n'umuguzi yabibonye.
  • Nta konte isabwa ku muguzi — akanda link gusa, yishyura kuri MoMo, birangiye.
  • Umucuruzi abona icyizere — abaguzi bamubona nk'umucuruzi wizewe kubera akoresha escrow.
  • Dispute resolution — niba hari ikibazo, TandPay igufasha kukikemura.
  • Code y'imibare 4 — uburyo bworoshye bwo kwemeza ko ibicuruzwa byageze.

Ibibazo bikunze kubazwa (FAQ)

Ese nkeneye konte kuri TandPay kugira ngo ngure?

Oya! Nk'umuguzi, ntukeneye konte. Ukanda link umucuruzi yakoherereje, ureba ibicuruzwa, wishyura kuri MoMo — birangiye. Nta app yo gushyiramo, nta izina ryo kwiyandikisha.

Ese amafaranga yanjye arizewe niba umucuruzi atantumiriye ibicuruzwa?

Yego! Amafaranga yawe afatwa na TandPay mu mutekano. Niba umucuruzi atakoze delivery, ushobora gufungura dispute, kandi TandPay izagufasha gusubizwa amafaranga.

Ese umucuruzi abona amafaranga ryari?

Umucuruzi abona amafaranga igihe umuguzi yanditse code y'imibare 4, byemeza ko ibicuruzwa byageze. Niba umuguzi atagize ikibazo mu masaha 12, amafaranga nawo arekurirwa mu buryo bw'ibisanzwe.

Ese TandPay ikora ku bintu byose?

TandPay ikora ku bicuruzwa byose bicuruzwa kuri social media mu Rwanda — imyenda, phones, ibikoresho by'amazu, ibindi byinshi. Ikora neza cyane ku bintu birenze 5,000 RWF aho umuguzi akeneye kurindwa.

Ni MoMo gusa ishobora gukoreshwa?

Kuri ubu, TandPay ikoresha MTN MoMo gusa. Mu gihe kiri imbere, tuzongera uburyo bundi bwo kwishyura.
