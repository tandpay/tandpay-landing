Buri $1 Yatakaye Kubera Uburiganya Igutwara $4.60 — TandPay Ikurinda

Yanditswe na Irene Bahati · 4 Werurwe 2026 · Gusoma: 9 min

Iyo abantu batekereza ku buriganya, batekereza ku mafaranga yibwe. Umuntu yakwibye 10,000 RWF — igihombo ni 10,000 RWF. Nibyo? Oya rwose.

Ubushakashatsi bwa LexisNexis Risk Solutions (2024) bwerekana ikintu giteye ubwoba: mu bihugu by'Afurika yo munsi y'Ubutayu bwa Sahara, buri dolar imwe ($1) yatakaye kubera uburiganya igutwara dolar $4.60 mu giciro nyacyo. Ibyo ni inshuro 4.60 z'amafaranga yibwe!

Kubera iki? Kubera ko uburiganya ntabwo butwara amafaranga gusa. Butwara n'ibindi byinshi — igihe, ibicuruzwa, abakiriya, n'icyizere. Muri iyi nkuru, tugiye kukwereka igiciro nyacyo cy'uburiganya, n'uburyo TandPay igufasha kwirinda.

$4.60 — Bituruka he?

$4.60 igiciro nyacyo cya buri $1 yatakaye (LexisNexis 2024)
84% by'abakoresha MM barigeze gukorerwa uburiganya (BNR/CGAP)
44% by'abahohotewe batakaje amafaranga (BNR/CGAP)

Reka dufate urugero rwo mu buzima bwa buri munsi. Tekereza umugore ucuruza imyenda kuri WhatsApp mu Kigali. Agurishije robe ku mafaranga 20,000 RWF. Yayitumiye ku muguzi. Umuguzi akayibona, ariko akavuga ko atarayibonye — akasaba kuyisubizwa amafaranga. Umucuruzi yaratakaje.

Ariko igihombo si 20,000 RWF gusa. Reka tubibare:

  • Igiciro cy'ibicuruzwa: 20,000 RWF — robe yahindutse. Ntishobora kongera gugurishwa nk'igishya.
  • Amafaranga ya transport: 2,000 RWF — yishyuye delivery.
  • Igihe cy'amasaha 3: ~3,000 RWF — igihe yakoresheje aganira n'umuguzi, ategura ibicuruzwa, agira delivery. Icyo gihe yari gukoresha acuruza abandi.
  • Abakiriya batakaye: ~15,000 RWF — kubera uburiganya, yatakaje umwete wo gucuruza kuri internet mu cyumweru kimwe. Byaba byatumye atakaza commande 2-3 z'abandi baguzi.
  • Reputation damage: ntibigereranywa — niba iyi nkuru yarangiye mu mucuruzi avuga ku bandi ko "gucuruza kuri WhatsApp bigoye," abakiriya bakaba bashya bataza.

Igiteranyo: hafi ya 40,000-50,000 RWF y'igihombo ku bintu bya 20,000 RWF gusa. Ni nk'uko LexisNexis ibivuga: inshuro 2-2.5 z'amafaranga yibwe (kandi ibyo ni mu mibare iri hasi — mu by'ukuri birashobora kuba biri hejuru).

Igiciro nyacyo cy'uburiganya mu Rwanda

Ubushakashatsi bwa BNR na CGAP bwerekana ko 84% by'abakoresha mobile money mu Rwanda barigeze gukorerwa uburiganya ubwo ari bwo bwose. Muri bo, 44% batakaje amafaranga nyayo. Ibyo bisobanura ko miliyoni z'Abanyarwanda bahohotewe — kandi igiciro nyacyo cy'ubwo buriganya kirenze cyane ibyo batekereza.

Uburiganya mu Rwanda: Imibare nyayo

Reka turebe uburiganya mu Rwanda mu mibare:

  • 84% by'abakoresha mobile money barigeze gukorerwa uburiganya (BNR/CGAP). Ibyo ni hafi ya buri muntu wese ukoresha MoMo.
  • 44% batakaje amafaranga nyayo (BNR/CGAP). Hafi kimwe cya kabiri by'abahohotewe bagize igihombo ry'amafaranga.
  • RIB yafashe umurongo w'uburiganya wagurishaga amafaranga arenze RWF 400,000,000 (miliyoni 400) muri SIM swap fraud (RIB 2024). Umurongo umwe gusa — tekereza uko hari iyindi.
  • 22% by'ibicuruzwa kuri Jumia (isoko nini rya e-commerce mu Afurika) bisubizwa/bihagarikwa kubera uburiganya n'ibibazo by'icyizere (Jumia SEC Filing).
  • BNR igamije kugabanya uburiganya 60% muri gahunda yayo ya 2026 (BNR Strategic Plan).

Iyi mibare yerekana ikintu kimwe: uburiganya ni ikibazo gikomeye mu Rwanda — kandi igiciro cyacyo ni kinini kurusha uko abantu babitekereza.

Igiciro cyacyo ku bacuruzi bato

Ku bacuruzi banini nka Jumia, uburiganya ni ikibazo cy'imibare — bashobora kubyihanganira kubera bafite amafaranga menshi. Ariko ku bacuruzi bato — abagore bacuruza imyenda kuri WhatsApp, abagabo bacuruza phones kuri Instagram, abantu bacuruza ibikoresho by'amazu kuri Facebook — uburiganya bumwe gusa bushobora kubatera igihombo gikomeye.

Dore uko igiciro cy'uburiganya gihinduka ku bacuruzi bato:

1. Gutakaza ibicuruzwa

Umucuruzi muto ntafite stock nyinshi. Niba yagurishije phone ya 80,000 RWF kandi akayohereza ku muguzi w'umuriganya, yatakaje phone n'amafaranga byombi. Ntashobora gusimbuza vuba — amafaranga ye ntayo.

2. Gutakaza igihe

Umucuruzi muto akoresha ubwe: ni we ukora marketing, ni we uganira n'abakiriya, ni we ugira delivery. Buri saha yashesheje ku muguzi w'umuriganya ni saha atari gucuruza. Igihe ni amafaranga.

3. Gutakaza abakiriya

Iyo umucuruzi muto agize uburiganya, hari igihe ahagarika gucuruza kuri internet mu gihe gito. Ibyo bisobanura ko abaguzi bashya batamubona — kandi ntibashobora kugura ibintu bye.

4. Gutakaza umwete

Iki ni igiciro kinini kuruta ibindi byose. Iyo umucuruzi ariganyijwe, amafaranga y'ubwoba yinjira mu bikorwa byose. Atangira gutinya abaguzi bose. Atangira kwima ibicuruzwa. Atangira gutekereza ko gucuruza kuri internet bigoye. Ubwo bwoba butwara amafaranga menshi kurusha uburiganya ubwabwo.

Urugero: Igiciro nyacyo ku mucuruzi w'imyenda

Umugore ucuruza imyenda ariganyijwe inshuro imwe — robe ya 20,000 RWF. Igiciro nyacyo: 20,000 (robe) + 2,000 (transport) + 3,000 (igihe) + 15,000 (abakiriya batakaye) = 40,000 RWF. Ariko niba yahagaritse gucuruza mu cyumweru kimwe kubera ubwoba, igihombo ny'amafaranga atakaye kirashobora kuba 100,000+ RWF. Ibyo byose kubera uburiganya bumwe gusa bwa 20,000 RWF.

TandPay ikurinda — mbere y'uko uburiganya bubaho

Ikintu cy'ingenzi cyane ugomba kumenya ni iki: TandPay ntabwo ikemura uburiganya nyuma y'uko bubaye. Ibubuza kubaho mu buryo bwa mbere.

Imibare: TandPay vs. Uburiganya

Reka tubibare ku rugero rwo mu buzima bwa buri munsi:

  • Ugurisha ibicuruzwa bya 20,000 RWF kuri TandPay.
  • Amafaranga ya TandPay: 20,000 x 2.5% = 500 RWF
  • Igiciro cy'uburiganya ku 20,000 RWF: 20,000 x 4.60 = 92,000 RWF (igiciro nyacyo)
  • Itandukaniro: 92,000 / 500 = 184x

Ibyo bisobanura ko escrow ya TandPay ihendutse inshuro 184 kurusha igiciro nyacyo cy'uburiganya. Kwishyura 500 RWF kugira ngo urinde 20,000 RWF ni amafaranga make cyane — ni nko kwishyura insurance y'ubucuruzi bwawe.

Kwirinda uburiganya ntabwo ari igishimishije gusa — ni amafaranga make cyane ugereranyije. TandPay ya 2.5% ihendutse inshuro 184 kurusha igiciro nyacyo cy'uburiganya bwa $4.60/$1.

Uburiganya burashobora kugabanuka — Rwanda irimo kubikora

Ubu butumwa si ubwo kwiheba. Rwanda irimo gukora ibintu bikomeye ku gukemura uburiganya:

  • BNR igamije kugabanya uburiganya 60% muri gahunda yayo y'umutekano wa digitale (BNR 2026).
  • RIB irimo gufata abagambanyi — umurongo w'uburiganya bwa RWF 400M+ wafashwe mu 2024.
  • MTN irimo gushyiraho umutekano mushya ku MoMo — biometric verification n'ibindi.
  • Amategeko mashya y'ubucuruzi bwa digitale arimo gutegurwa na RURA.

Ariko amategeko n'ubushakashatsi bifata igihe. Ubu, umucuruzi wese n'umuguzi wese ashobora kwirinda mu buryo bworoshye: gukoresha escrow. Ntabwo usabwa gutegereza amategeko — ushobora gukoresha TandPay ubu nyine.
