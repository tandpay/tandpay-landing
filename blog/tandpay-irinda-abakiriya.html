TandPay irinda abakiriya n'abacuruzi: Uko bikora

Na Irene Bahati · 19 Mutarama 2026 · Iminota 8 yo gusoma

Ikibazo: Kohereza amafaranga kuri MoMo ntacyo birinda

Mu Rwanda, abantu benshi bacuruza kuri WhatsApp, Instagram na TikTok. Umucuruzi ashyira ibifoto by'ibicuruzwa, umuguzi arabona, baravugana, hanyuma umuguzi yohereza amafaranga kuri MTN MoMo. Ariko hari ikibazo gikomeye: nyuma yo kohereza amafaranga, ntacyo birinda umuguzi.

Niba umucuruzi adakoze neza — ntiyohereze ibintu, cyangwa yohereze ibintu bidahura n'ibyo wari warategetse — amafaranga yawe yagiye. Nta buryo bworoshye bwo kuyasubiza. MoMo ubwayo ni uburyo bwo kohereza amafaranga gusa, ntabwo ari uburyo bwo gukora ubucuruzi bwizewe.

Ikibazo cy'ingenzi

Ubushakashatsi bwerekana ko abaguzi 4 kuri 10 mu Rwanda batinya gucuruza online kubera ko baba bazi neza ko nta buryo bwo gusubizwa amafaranga iyo ibintu bitagenze neza. Ibi bibuza ubucuruzi online gukura mu Rwanda.

Ibi bitera ibibazo byinshi: abakiriya batinya gutanga amafaranga mbere, abacuruzi nabo batinya kohereza ibintu mbere yo kwishyurwa. Ntawuzi niba undi azakorana neza. Icyo ni cyo TandPay yaremejwe gukemura.

Igisubizo: TandPay ifata amafaranga yawe mu mutekano

TandPay ni platfome ya escrow ya mbere mu Rwanda yagenewe ubucuruzi bwo kuri social media. Escrow mu Rwanda ni ikintu gishya — kandi TandPay ni yo yayizanye mu gucuruza kuri social media. "Escrow" bisobanuye ko hari umuntu w'inyagati — TandPay — ufata amafaranga y'umuguzi, akayafata mu mutekano kugeza ubwo umucuruzi yohereje ibintu kandi umuguzi yabyemeje.

Mu magambo yoroshye: ntawe uhomba. Umuguzi azi ko amafaranga ye atazigera ajya ku mucuruzi mbere yuko abona ibintu bye. Umucuruzi na we azi ko amafaranga amaze gutangwa — ntacyo bikwiye kumuteza impungenge.

Uko bikora intambwe ku yindi

Reka dusuzume buri ntambwe y'ubucuruzi kuri TandPay, kuva mu gutangira kugeza ku gihe amafaranga arekurirwa umucuruzi.

Intambwe ya 1: Umucuruzi akora ibicuruzwa cyangwa commande kuri TandPay

Umucuruzi wiyandikishije kuri TandPay yinjira kuri app ye, agashyiraho ibicuruzwa bye: izina, ibiciro, amafoto, n'ibisobanuro. Ashobora no gukora commande idasanzwe (custom order) iyo umuguzi amaze kumubwira ibyo ashaka. Buri kintu gifite page yacyo yihariye kuri TandPay.

Intambwe ya 2: Agasangiza link y'ubwishyu kuri WhatsApp, Instagram cyangwa TikTok

Iyo ibicuruzwa bimaze gushyirwa, umucuruzi abona link idasanzwe y'ubwishyu ashobora gusangiza abakiriya be aho ari hose — kuri WhatsApp, Instagram DMs, TikTok, cyangwa urundi rubuga. Umuguzi ntakenera kuba afite app ya TandPay — akanda link gusa.

Ikintu cyiza: Umuguzi ntakenera kugira konti

TandPay yubatswe ku buryo umuguzi adakenera kugura app, kwiyandikisha, cyangwa gukora konti. Akanda link, areba ibicuruzwa, yishyura kuri MoMo, arangiza. Ni byoroshye cyane.

Intambwe ya 3: Umuguzi akanda link, areba ibicuruzwa, amakuru y'umucuruzi, na reviews

Iyo umuguzi akanze link, abona page irimo amakuru yose akeneye: ifoto y'ibicuruzwa, igiciro, ibisobanuro, na — ikintu cy'ingenzi — profil y'umucuruzi irimo izina, trust score, n'ibitekerezo (reviews) by'abakiriya bamaze gucuruza na we.

Ibi bituma umuguzi ashobora gufata icyemezo azi neza uwo acuruza na we. Niba umucuruzi afite trust score nziza na reviews nziza, umuguzi yumva yizeye. Niba ari umucuruzi mushya utaraba na review, umuguzi azamenya ko akwiye kwitonda.

Intambwe ya 4: Umuguzi yandika numero ye ya MTN MoMo, akanda "Ishyura"

Iyo umuguzi amaze kwemera ibyo agura, yandika numero ye ya telefone ya MTN (numero ikoresha MoMo). Nta makuru yandi akenera gutanga — nta izina, nta email, nta password. Numero ya telefone gusa. Hanyuma akanda buto ya "Ishyura".

Intambwe ya 5: MoMo imwohereza USSD prompt, yandika PIN ye

Nyuma yo gukanda "Ishyura", TandPay yohereza iyifuzwa kuri MTN MoMo API. MoMo yohereza USSD push kuri telefone y'umuguzi — ni uwo message ukunda kugaragara ugusaba kwemeza ubwishyu. Umuguzi areba amafaranga, areba izina ry'umucuruzi, hanyuma yandika PIN ye ya MoMo kugira ngo yemeze.

Umutekano wa PIN yawe

PIN yawe ya MoMo ntabwo TandPay iyibona. Ubwishyu bwose bunyura kuri MTN MoMo API ikomeye. TandPay ibona gusa niba ubwishyu bwakunze cyangwa butakunze — ntabwo ibona PIN yawe na rimwe.

Intambwe ya 6: Amafaranga afatwa na TandPay mu mutekano — NTABWO ajya ku mucuruzi

Aha ni ho escrow itangira. Amafaranga ntabwo ajya ku mucuruzi. Ahubwo, afatwa na TandPay mu konte y'umutekano. Ahagarara aho kugeza ubwo intambwe zikurikira zirangiye. Umucuruzi abona notification ko amafaranga yamaze gutangwa, ariko ntashobora kuyakoresha — arafatwa mu mutekano.

Iki ni cyo gikomeye cyane mu buryo bwa TandPay. Mu bucuruzi busanzwe kuri MoMo, amafaranga aba amaze kujya ku mucuruzi — nta buryo bwo kuyasubiza. Kuri TandPay, amafaranga aracyafatwa na platfome kugeza umuguzi avuze ko yishimye.

Intambwe ya 7: Umucuruzi abimenyeshwa, ahabwa code y'imibare 4

Umucuruzi abona notification kuri app ye ya TandPay imubwira ko umuguzi yamaze kwishyura. Mu notification, abona kandi code y'imibare 4 (urugero: 7291). Iyi code ni ikimenyetso cy'uko ibintu byageze ku muguzi — ni nk'igihamya cy'uko delivery yarakoze.

Intambwe ya 8: Umucuruzi ashyikiriza ibicuruzwa, atanga code ku muguzi

Umucuruzi yohereza ibicuruzwa ku muguzi — ashobora kubigeza iwe, bakabonana ahantu, cyangwa akoresheje service yo kohereza. Igihe ibintu bigeze ku muguzi, umucuruzi amubwira iyo code y'imibare 4. Code irashobora kuba ku mpapuro yanditse, kuri message, cyangwa mu magambo.

Intambwe ya 9: Umuguzi yandika code kuri TandPay akamenyesha ko yabonye ibintu

Umuguzi yinjira kuri link ya TandPay (cyangwa SMS yakiriye), yandika iyo code y'imibare 4. Ibi bisobanura ko umuguzi yemeza ko yabonye ibintu bye kandi yishimye. Ni nk'uko wahaye igikumu ku bintu byoherejwe.

Kuki code y'imibare 4 ari ingenzi

Code y'imibare 4 ni ikimenyetso kidashidikanywa. Ntawe ushobora kuvuga ko atabonye ibintu niba yamaze kwandika code. Birakemura ibibazo byinshi by'impaka hagati y'umucuruzi n'umuguzi.

Intambwe ya 10: Amafaranga arekurirwa umucuruzi (hasigaye amafaranga ya serivisi)

Iyo umuguzi amaze kwandika code yemeza ko yabonye ibintu, TandPay irekura amafaranga yose ku konte ya MoMo y'umucuruzi. Hasigaye gusa amafaranga make ya serivisi ya TandPay (2.5% ku mucuruzi). Umucuruzi abona amafaranga ye kuri MoMo ye vuba cyane.

Ubucuruzi bwarangiye neza! Umuguzi yabonye ibintu bye, umucuruzi yabonye amafaranga ye, nta wundi uhombye.

Iyo hatabaye ikibazo?

Mu buzima busanzwe, ibintu ntabwo buri gihe bijya neza. Hari igihe umuguzi adashimishwa n'ibintu yakiriye, cyangwa ibintu bigatinda. TandPay ifite uburyo bwo gukemura ibibazo nk'ibi:

  • Umuguzi yanga ibintu: Niba umuguzi abonye ibintu bidahura n'ibyo yari yarategetse, ashobora gufungura ikibazo (dispute) kuri TandPay mu masaha 12 nyuma yo guhabwa ibintu. Amafaranga akomeza gufatwa mu mutekano.
  • Admin isuzuma: Itsinda rya TandPay risuzuma ibimenyetso — amafoto, messages, n'ibindi — kugira ngo bamenye uwufite ukuri.
  • Igisubizo: Admin ishobora kwemeza ko amafaranga asubizwa umuguzi (refund) cyangwa arekurirwa umucuruzi (release), bitewe n'ibimenyetso.
  • Ibimenyetso: Bombi — umucuruzi n'umuguzi — bashobora gutanga ibimenyetso birimo amafoto, amajwi, na messages kugira ngo bemeze ibyo bavuga.

Igihe cy'amasaha 12

Umuguzi afite amasaha 12 gusa nyuma yo kwemeza ko yabonye ibintu kugira ngo afungure ikibazo. Nyuma y'ayo masaha, amafaranga arekurirwa umucuruzi by'ukuri. Ibi bikora kugira ngo umucuruzi na we abone amafaranga ye vuba.

Amafaranga ya serivisi ya TandPay

TandPay ikoresha uburyo bworoshye bw'amafaranga ya serivisi:

  • Umuguzi yishyura 2.5% by'igiciro cy'ibicuruzwa nk'amafaranga yo kurindwa (buyer protection fee)
  • Umucuruzi yishyura 2.5% by'igiciro cy'ibicuruzwa nk'amafaranga ya platfome (platform fee)

Urugero: Niba ibicuruzwa bifite igiciro cya 10,000 RWF:

  • Umuguzi yishyura: 10,000 + 250 (2.5%) = 10,250 RWF
  • Umucuruzi ahabwa: 10,000 - 250 (2.5%) = 9,750 RWF
  • TandPay ibona: 250 + 250 = 500 RWF (5% y'igiciro)

Ibi ni bisanzwe cyane ugereranyije n'ibiciro by'ubundi buryo bwo kwishyura. Kandi ayo mafaranga make atuma umuguzi n'umucuruzi bombi barindwa — ni igiciro gito cy'umutekano.

Umutekano w'amakuru yawe

Umutekano wo kwishyura ni ikintu cy'ibanze kuri TandPay. TandPay yita ku mutekano w'amakuru y'abakiriya n'abacuruzi:

  • Encryption: Amakuru yose ajya kuri TandPay akingiwe na encryption ikomeye (HTTPS/TLS). Ntawe ushobora gusoma amakuru yawe mu nzira.
  • PIN hashing: PIN y'umucuruzi ifatwa mu buryo budasomeka (hashing). Naho umuntu yakwinjira mu database ya TandPay, ntashobora kubona PIN yawe.
  • Amakuru y'abantu: TandPay ntabwo isangiza amakuru y'abakiriya na rubanda. Numero ya telefone y'umuguzi ibonwa n'umucuruzi gusa ku bw'ubucuruzi bwabo.
  • Row Level Security: Buri mucuruzi abona amakuru ye gusa — ntashobora kubona amakuru y'abandi bacuruzi kuri platfome.

Ni nde ukoresha TandPay?

TandPay yagenewe abantu bose bacuruza kuri social media mu Rwanda:

  • Abacuruzi ba WhatsApp: Abagurisha imyenda, ibikoresho by'amazu, telefone, n'ibindi kuri WhatsApp groups na status.
  • Abacuruzi ba Instagram: Boutique z'imyenda, ibijeni (jewelry), ibikoresho by'uburanga (beauty products) — bose bakoresha TandPay kugira ngo abakiriya babo bishyure mu mutekano.
  • Abacuruzi ba TikTok: Ababoneka kuri TikTok Live bagurisha ibicuruzwa — bagira link ya TandPay mu bio yabo.
  • Abakiriya bose: Umuntu wese ufite MTN MoMo ashobora kwishyura kuri TandPay — ntakenera app, ntakenera konti.

Impamvu TandPay itandukanye n'ibindi

Hari ibintu bitandukanira TandPay n'ubundi buryo bwo gucuruza online:

  • Ntawe ukenera app: Umuguzi akoresha browser ye gusa — nta download, nta install.
  • MTN MoMo gusa: Nta karita ya banki ikenwa. Niba ufite MoMo, ushobora gukoresha TandPay.
  • Byubatswe mu Rwanda, ku b'Abanyarwanda: TandPay yubatswe na ba tech n'Abanyarwanda, ishyira imbere ibibazo by'ubucuruzi bw'i Rwanda n'i Kigali.
  • Umuguzi ntakenera konti: Mu bundi buryo, umuguzi akeneye kugura konti, kwandika izina, email, na password. Kuri TandPay, numero ya telefone gusa ihagije.

Ibibazo bikunze kubazwa

Ese nshobora gukoresha TandPay ku yindi telefone itari ya MTN?

Ku bw'ubu, TandPay ikoresha MTN MoMo gusa ku bw'ubwishyu. Ariko ushobora kureba ibicuruzwa kuri telefone iyo ari yo yose — ugura ufite MoMo gusa.

Ese amafaranga yanjye ashobora kuzimira?

Oya. Amafaranga afatwa na TandPay mu konte y'umutekano. Niba ikibazo kibonetse, TandPay ishobora kuyasubiza ku muguzi cyangwa kuyohereza ku mucuruzi — ariko ntabwo azigera azimira.

Ni ryari umucuruzi abona amafaranga?

Umucuruzi abona amafaranga ye vuba nyuma yuko umuguzi yanditse code y'imibare 4 yemeza ko yabonye ibintu. Niba nta kibazo, amafaranga ajya kuri MoMo y'umucuruzi muri minote nke.

Ese nshobora guhagarika ubwishyu?

Nyuma yuko ubwishyu bwemejwe kuri MoMo, ntushobora kubuhagarika. Ariko amafaranga afatwa mu mutekano na TandPay — ntabwo ajya ku mucuruzi kugeza wemeje ko wabonye ibintu byawe.

Tangira gukoresha TandPay uyu munsi

Niba uri umucuruzi, iyandikishe kuri app.tandpay.com kugira ngo utangire gukoresha TandPay. Uzashyiraho ibicuruzwa byawe, ubone link y'ubwishyu, kandi utangire gucuruza mu mutekano.

Niba uri umuguzi, ntacyo ukeneye gukora — urategereza gusa ko umucuruzi wawe akohereza link ya TandPay. Iyo ubonye link ya TandPay, umenye ko amafaranga yawe arindwa.
